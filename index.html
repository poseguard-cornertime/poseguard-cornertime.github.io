Important announcement

Offline and Online APK flavors

PoseGuard is now distributed as two separate APK flavors. Choose the version that matches how you want to use the app.

Choose your version

Offline

Offline APK

Keeps the previous behavior unchanged. It does not request the Android INTERNET permission and continues to run fully on-device.

Online

Online APK

Adds integration with external toys through Intiface Central / buttplug.io. This build requests the Android INTERNET permission only to communicate with Intiface Central and send control signals to connected devices.

Online features

Vibrate devices

Connect compatible Vibrate devices through Intiface Central / buttplug.io.

Pose-hold signals

Send vibration signals while the required pose is being held.

Violation signals

Send vibration signals when a violation is detected, configurable from the app settings.

Privacy

Processing stays on your device

Pose and face detection processing is performed locally on the device. PoseGuard does not upload images, logs, telemetry, pose data, or user data to external servers.

Current limitations

At this stage, only Vibration control is supported. Other device modes such as Oscillation, Rotation, and additional actuator types are not implemented yet.
